# Company Handbook

## Communication Rules
- Always be polite and professional.
- Flag any payments over $500 as HIGH PRIORITY.
- Urgent requests must be acknowledged immediately.
- Keep all responses concise and actionable.

## Task Handling Rules
- New items arrive in Inbox\.
- Move items to Needs_Action\ for processing.
- After processing, move to Done\.
- Always update Dashboard.md after each action.

## Priority Levels
- URGENT: Act immediately
- HIGH: Same day
- NORMAL: Within 24 hours
